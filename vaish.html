A.P.J Kalam The People President

THE LITTLE BOY ON THE BEACH

A short rather plain looking six years old boy ,stood on a sandy beach. As soon as the young boy returned home from school ,he would run to the beach and watch for hours . As he watched ,the seed of the future engineer were being sown. This boy was none other than A. P. J. Abdul Kalam.

EARLY CHILDHOOD

Kalam was born on october 15, 1931 in Rameshwaram, an island tucked away in the southermost tip of india . His father was a disciplined man and his , mother was a tall and good looking lady .kalam was her favourite child .

STUDENT LIFE

After four years of study ,Kalam obtained his Bachelors in Science degree later he decided to switch course and study aeronautical engineering and sent his application to India's premier.
